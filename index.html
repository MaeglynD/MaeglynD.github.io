Design

The design phase is arguably the most crucial step in the entire build process. Design leaks into absolutely everything, from developer motivation to user satisfaction, which is why photoshop will have my undivided attention until we have a site mockup to be more than proud of.

Apart from pretty, the design will also be considerate of development needs. With extensive knowledge of browser capabilities and 3+ years experience working with responsive UI & UX design I’ll ensure the design is fully transmutable from start to finish.

Development

Developing a high quality website requires hands on, practical experience from all paths of development. Front to back, I’ll ensure exactly that, without compromising performance, appearance or security

Additionally, I develop with a emphasis on comprehensibility, to achieve clear, effective communication with other developers. I also have no problem applying my skill set to various challenging issues that go outside my designated domain, API problems? Hardware issues? I’ve got you covered.
